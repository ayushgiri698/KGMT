ABOUT US

Our Diploma in Medicine Assistant Certification program is designed for MBBS/MD graduates seeking to enhance their skills as medical assistants. The comprehensive curriculum covers medical terminology, patient care techniques, procedures, pharmacology, documentation, and ethics. Experienced faculty members provide guidance and share practical insights. Clinical rotations and internships offer hands-on experience in diverse medical settings. Upon completion, graduates are equipped to assist physicians, perform procedures, manage records, and provide compassionate patient care. Join our program to advance your career as a qualified and skilled medical assistant
